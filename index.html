Introduction

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a 2017 action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. The game is an installment of The Legend of Zelda series and is set at the end of its timeline. The player controls an amnesiac Link, who awakens from a hundred-year slumber, and attempts to regain his memories and prevent the destruction of Hyrule by Calamity Ganon. The world in this game features minimal structure and is designed to encourage exploration and experimentation, and the main story quest can be completed in a nonlinear fashion.

Features

Discover the vast world around you

Explore the landscape of Hyrule any way you like. Climb up towers and mountain peaks in search of new destinations, then set your own path and plunge into the wilderness. With this much real estate to explore, Link will need some help in his travels. Luckily, there are plenty of transportation options to unlock. Link can cover a lot of ground quickly on horseback, navigate the waters of Hyrule on a raft, or hit the slopes and surf down a mountain on his shield. And the sky's the limit when Links soars through the sky with his paraglider.

Fight for your Kingdom

When Link first wakes up, he's unarmed and defenseless. Relying on his wits and resourcefulness, though, he can acquire over 180 different weapons to use in combat. These items will wear out over time, though, so he must always be on the lookout for more. There's more to fighting than just weapons, of course. Link will also have to learn a variety of different attacks—like high-flying bow shots and making use of explosives—in order to prevail.

Characters

Link

Link is the main character and protagonist of the The Legend of Zelda series, usually depicted as an ordinary boy who becomes a legendary hero by saving the world through feats of courage.

Zelda

In Breath of the Wild, Zelda is not only the princess of Hyrule, she is also an aspiring scholar who is very curious. She is also the leader of the Champions of Hyrule.

Mipha

The Princess of the Zora, a friend of Link, and one of the Champions. She is described as being introverted and has a gift for healing.

Daruk

The warrior Goron Champion, tasked with piloting the Divine Beasts in the war against Calamity Ganon 100 years ago. He is generally calm, but becomes wildly excitable in battle.

Urbosa

A Gerudo Champion who pilots the Divine Beast Vah Naboris. She is capable of manipulating lightning using her technique, Urbosa's Fury.

Revali

The Champion of the Rito tribe. Known as the most skilled archer of all the Rito. Revali is known for his arrogant, sardonic outward personality.
